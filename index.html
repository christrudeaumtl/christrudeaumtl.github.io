I study noise and sound in urban space. I ask whether (and which) groups are more exposed to noise or have less access spaces that have sounds they enjoy hearing. I'm also interested in the attitudes people have toward noise.

During my PhD at McGill University, I worked on a number of urban sound projects across Montréal. I also have some of my publications listed on this site.
