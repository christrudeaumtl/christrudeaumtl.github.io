--- layout: home title: Hi, I'm Chris subtitle: Social sciences researcher --- I study noise and sound in urban space. I ask whether (and which) groups are more exposed to noise or have less access spaces that have sounds they enjoy hearing. I'm also interested in the attitudes people have toward noise. During my PhD at McGill University, I worked on a number of urban sound projects across Montréal. Affiliations - [Multimodal Interaction Lab](https://mil.mcgill.ca/) - [Sounds in the City](https://www.sounds-in-the-city.org/) - [Center for Interdisciplinary Research on Montréal (CIRM)](https://www.mcgill.ca/centre-montreal/) - [Réseau Air, Intersectorialité, Recherche Respiratoire ET Sonore (AIRS)](https://airs.quebec/)
